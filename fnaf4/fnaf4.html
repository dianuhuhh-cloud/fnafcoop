THE FINAL CHAPTER

WHAT HAVE YOU BROUGHT HOME?

ABOUT THE GAME

This time, the terror has followed you home. In this final chapter of the Five Nights at Freddy's original story, you must once again defend yourself against Freddy Fazbear, Bonnie, Chica, Foxy, and even worse things that lurk in the shadows. Watching only your flashlight and your hearing, you must survive until 6 AM by watching the doors, as well as warding off unwanted creatures that may venture into your closet or onto the bed behind you.

ANIMATRONICS

NIGHTMARE FREDDY

Check the bed behind you frequently. Use your flashlight to scare away the 'Freddles' before they summon the Freddy.
