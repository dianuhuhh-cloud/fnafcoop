ABOUT THE GAME

Freddy Fazbear's Pizza Place is under new management — yours. As the freshly hired franchise owner, your job is simple: stock the restaurant, purchase equipment from four different catalogs, and build the most successful Freddy Fazbear location this side of town.

Each catalog offers something different. Dumpster Diver Weekly for budget finds. Stan's Budget Tech for practical equipment. Smiles and Servos for entertainment upgrades. And Rare Finds Auction for the premium stuff, if you can afford it.

Keep your Atmosphere, Health and Safety, and Entertainment scores high. Keep the customers happy. Keep the revenue up. Open on Monday. Close on Friday. Do not ask questions about what happens after Friday.

ABOUT THE GAME

The workday ends at midnight. After the last employee leaves, you remain at the terminal. There are two vents. There is a fan you can turn off to reduce noise, and a PC you can shut down for the same reason. The temperature rises when you do both.

Something was found in the back alley. Management decided it was worth recovering. You did not make that call but you are the one sitting alone with it now. Five audio cues. A taser with three charges. A clipboard for documentation that nobody will ever read.

Complete the session. File the form. Do not let it leave the room.

ROLES

NIGHT GUARD

Stationed at the back office after closing. Watch the two vents and manage noise by killing the fan and the PC when things get loud. The temperature climbs when you do both. Complete each night, file the form, and do not let anything reach the door.

JANITOR

Walk the halls of the Pizzeria during open hours. Keep the place presentable, clean up after the customers, and stay on your route. The building is bigger than it looks from the outside. Do not stray too far from where you are supposed to be.

ENGINEER

You run the salvage procedure. When something gets dragged in from the back alley, you are the one in the room with it. Five audio cues. A clipboard nobody will read. Whatever came through that door responds to sound, and right now you are the only one making any.

POLICEMAN

You sit at the terminal in the office and work the systems. Cameras, audio routing, motion alerts, all of it runs through your screen. When something moves that should not be moving, you are the first to know. Click fast. You do not get many chances.
