FREDDY FAZBEAR'S PIZZA GRAND REOPENING

WELCOME TO THE NEW AND IMPROVED FREDDY FAZBEAR'S PIZZA

ABOUT THE GAME

Welcome to the new and improved Freddy Fazbear's Pizza! You're the new security guard working the night shift. There are no doors this time to protect you... just an empty Freddy Fazbear head to fool the animatronics, and a Music Box you must keep wound up. Survive five nights as the new cast joins the old, withered ones.

CHARACTERS

TOY FREDDY

The new and improved face of Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. Approaches through the main hallway. Put on the Freddy mask when he enters the office.

DISCOVER

ROLES

NIGHTGUARD

Watch the cameras, keep the music box wound, use your flashlight, and put on the Freddy head when they get too close. No doors this time. Just you and the dark until 6 AM.

JANITOR

Clean up the party rooms after the crowds go home. The kids leave a mess, the building makes noise, and something in Parts & Service doesn't sound right tonight.

ENGINEER

Keep the new facial recognition systems from going offline and fix what breaks before it becomes a problem. Stay sharp. The Mangle has a habit of showing up where it shouldn't.

POLICEMAN

Brought in to look into the rumors surrounding the grand reopening. Help the night guard keep things under control while you figure out what's really going on inside these walls.
