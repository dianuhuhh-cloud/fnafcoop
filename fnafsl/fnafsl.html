Five Nights at Freddy's

Circus Baby's Entertainment and Rental

WELCOME TO CIRCUS BABY'S PIZZA WORLD

ABOUT THE GAME

You have been given the prestigious opportunity to work the night shift at a brand-new Afton Robotics facility. Unlike anything seen before, the animatronics here are something special — Funtime Freddy, Ballora, Funtime Foxy, and Circus Baby. Follow HandUnit's instructions carefully, navigate the underground facility, and survive each shift. Something is wrong here. The animatronics have been behaving... oddly. Stay calm. You'll be fine.

NIGHTS

N1

THE ELEVATOR

Descend into the underground facility for the first time. Follow HandUnit's directives through the Primary Control Module. Learn the layout. Begin your first shift.

N2

THE BREAKER ROOM

Return to the facility for your second shift. Crawl beneath the desk to hide from certain danger. Navigate Ballora’s Gallery to each the Breaker Room to manually restore power to the building while staying one step ahead of Funtime Freddy.

N3

PARTS & SERVICE

Venture deeper into the facility. Sneak through the corridors of Funtime Auditorium using your flash beacon to avoid Funtime Foxy to reach Parts and Service to carefully perform maintenance on Funtime Freddy, and try to make it back.

N4

THE SPRINGLOCK SUIT

You wake up trapped inside an old springlock suit. Listen closely to Baby's instructions to survive. Keep the deadly springlocks tightly wound while shaking off the swarm of Minireenas trying to climb inside with you.

N5

THE SCOOPING ROOM

Arrive for your final, fateful shift. Extract a crucial keycard from Circus Baby in Parts and Service. Follow her exact directions through Funtime Auditorium to reach the Scooping Room and uncover the truth of the facility.

N6

THE PRIVATE ROOM

Disobey the instructions and find your way into the facility's secret observation room. Use the classic camera system and security doors to defend yourself. Conserve your limited power and hold out until 6 AM against ennard.
