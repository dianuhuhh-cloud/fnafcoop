WELCOME TO FAZBEAR'S FRIGHT

ABOUT THE GAME

Thirty years after Freddy Fazbear's Pizza closed, the horror isn't gone. Fazbear's Fright is built from old props, costumes, and stories… and something real. As night guard, survive until 6 AM while cameras glitch, audio cuts out, and ventilation fails. And there's one animatronic. He moves when you are not looking. Welcome back.

CHARACTERS

SPRINGTRAP

A decayed rabbit suit hiding something far worse inside... and the only real threat.

DISCOVER

ROLES

NIGHTGUARD

Watch the cameras, keep the vents sealed, and use the audio lures to keep Springtrap away from your office. One animatronic. That's all it takes.

JANITOR

Keep the attraction running through the night while the building works against you. The corridors are dark, the phantoms aren't real, and something is moving in the vents.

ENGINEER

Ventilation down. Audio offline. Cameras cutting out. Fix what's failing before the systems collapse completely, and try not to think about what's crawling through the walls.

POLICEMAN

Called in when the reports stopped making sense. Patrol the restricted areas, look into what shouldn't be here, and keep the night guard alive long enough to get answers.
